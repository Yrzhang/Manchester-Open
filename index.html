Tournament Information

The Manchester Open is a 5-round McMahon tournament and a BGA Class A rated event.

  • 5-round McMahon tournament
  • Fischer timing: 45 minutes + 10 seconds per move
  • BGA Class A rated tournament
Venue

Luther King House
Brighton Grove, Manchester M14 5JP
https://lutherkinghouse.co.uk

Approximately 2 miles from Manchester Piccadilly Station.
Bus routes 41, 42, 43 and 142 connect to city centre.

Budget accommodation available at the venue (contact directly).

Prizes

  • 1st, 2nd and 3rd place overall
  • Best Under-16 player
  • Best player 10 kyu or lower
  • Best kyu player
  • Players with 4 or 5 wins

Category prizes are determined by number of wins, then final ranking.
