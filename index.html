Manchester Open Go Tournament 2026

15–16 August 2026

Venue

Luther King House

Brighton Grove, Manchester M14 5JP

https://lutherkinghouse.co.uk

The venue is approximately 2 miles from Manchester Piccadilly Station. Frequent bus services (41, 42, 43 and 142) connect the venue with Manchester city centre.

Budget accommodation is available at the venue. Please contact Luther King House directly for booking enquiries.

Tournament Format

  • 5-round McMahon tournament
  • Fischer timing: 45 minutes + 10 seconds per move
  • BGA Class A tournament

Refreshments

Complimentary tea, coffee and biscuits will be available throughout the tournament.

Prizes

  • 1st, 2nd and 3rd place overall
  • Best Under-16 player
  • Best player 10 kyu or lower
  • Best kyu player
  • All players achieving 4 or 5 wins

Ranking for age/kyu prizes is determined first by wins, then by final tournament placing.
